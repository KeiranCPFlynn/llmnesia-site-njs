Opening Gemini to a blank conversation sidebar — or finding that previous conversations seem to have vanished — is a common and usually solvable problem. In nearly every case, your conversations are intact. The issue is a loading or configuration problem, not data loss.

This guide covers every known cause and the fix for each, ordered from fastest to try to most involved.

## Step 1: Check for a Google service outage

Before investigating your own setup, confirm Google's services are running normally:

1. Go to **google.com/appsstatus** or the Google Workspace Status Dashboard
2. Check if Gemini or related services are listed as having an incident
3. Check @Google's social channels during significant outages for real-time updates

If there's an active Google service incident, your history will return once it's resolved. There's nothing to fix on your end.

## Step 2: Hard refresh the page

The fastest fix for most intermittent history loading issues:

- **Windows/Linux:** Ctrl + Shift + R
- **Mac:** Cmd + Shift + R

A hard refresh bypasses your browser's cached version of Gemini and forces a fresh load. Many cases where history appears blank resolve immediately with this step.

## Step 3: Verify your Gemini Apps Activity is enabled

This is a Gemini-specific cause that doesn't exist on other platforms. Gemini conversation history is only saved if your **Gemini Apps Activity** is enabled in your Google account:

1. Go to **myactivity.google.com**
2. Look for "Gemini Apps Activity" in the left panel or settings
3. Verify the toggle is set to "Saving" (on)
4. If it was turned off, re-enable it — note that conversations from when it was off were not saved and won't appear

If this setting was recently paused or turned off — either by you or by a Google account policy on a work/school account — that explains missing history.

**Managed accounts (school/work Google accounts):** Your organisation's admin controls may restrict Gemini history saving. Check with your IT administrator if you're on a managed Google Workspace account and history isn't being retained.

## Step 4: Check you're signed into the correct Google account

If you use multiple Google accounts — personal, work, school — Gemini history is per account. What appears as "missing history" is often history that exists on a different account:

1. Click your profile icon in the top-right corner of gemini.google.com
2. Verify which account is active
3. Switch accounts if needed
4. Check whether history loads under the correct account

## Step 5: Test in an incognito window

Browser extensions are a frequent cause of Gemini loading failures:

1. Open a new incognito window (Ctrl+Shift+N in Chrome, Ctrl+Shift+P in Firefox)
2. Go to gemini.google.com and sign in
3. Check whether your conversation history loads

**If history loads in incognito:** An extension is interfering. Continue to step 6.

**If history doesn't load in incognito:** The issue is not extension-related. Skip to step 7.

## Step 6: Isolate the conflicting extension

If incognito confirmed an extension is causing the problem:

1. Open chrome://extensions
2. Disable all extensions
3. Reload Gemini and verify history loads
4. Re-enable extensions one at a time, reloading after each
5. Identify which extension breaks history loading

Common extension categories that cause Gemini conflicts:
- **Ad blockers** — can block Google's API requests that Gemini's history loading depends on
- **Privacy tools** — may block cookies or requests the Gemini interface needs
- **Script blockers** — can prevent sidebar JavaScript from executing
- **Other Google account tools** — some extensions that manage multiple Google accounts interfere with Gemini session state

For Google-related conflicts, adding an exception for gemini.google.com in your extension is usually sufficient.

## Step 7: Clear Gemini's site data

Corrupted cached data can prevent the conversation sidebar from loading:

**Chrome:**
1. Click the padlock icon in the address bar on gemini.google.com
2. Select "Site settings"
3. Click "Clear data"
4. Sign back in with your Google account

**Firefox:**
1. Click the padlock icon
2. Select "Clear cookies and site data"
3. Sign back in

After clearing, allow 10–20 seconds for the conversation list to load after signing in.

## Step 8: Check the browser storage limit

On devices with limited storage or ChromeOS:

1. Go to chrome://settings/content/all
2. Search for "google.com" or "gemini.google.com"
3. If storage is unusually high, clear it and reload

This is uncommon on standard devices but can affect Chromebooks or shared managed computers.

## Step 9: Try a different browser

If none of the above has resolved the issue:

1. Open a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari)
2. Sign into gemini.google.com with your Google account
3. Check whether history loads

If it loads in the alternate browser, the issue is specific to your primary browser's installation. Resetting browser settings to defaults typically resolves this.

## Step 10: Contact Google support

For persistent issues:

1. Use the feedback option within the Gemini interface (the thumbs down or feedback icon)
2. Alternatively, go to support.google.com and search for Gemini-related help
3. Describe the issue, your browser, OS, and the steps you've already tried

## Gemini history: what to know about retention

Unlike ChatGPT or Claude, Gemini conversation history is tied directly to Google's My Activity system rather than a platform-specific history feature. This creates a few unique behaviors:

**Auto-delete settings:** Google's My Activity has configurable auto-delete policies. If you've set Google to auto-delete activity after 3 months or 18 months, Gemini conversations older than that threshold are automatically removed. You can check and adjust this at myactivity.google.com → Data and privacy → History settings.

**Gemini Advanced vs free tier:** Both tiers save conversation history the same way, tied to Gemini Apps Activity. The distinction is model capability, not history retention.

**Exported history:** If you want a copy of your Gemini history independent of Google's retention policies, you can export it via Google Takeout (takeout.google.com). Select "Gemini Apps Activity" to download your conversation history as JSON.

## Maintaining access when Gemini history is unreliable

LLMnesia indexes your Gemini conversations locally as you browse them. The index is stored on your device and remains accessible independently of Google's history system. If Gemini's native history fails to load — whether due to a loading error, account issue, or activity settings change — your indexed conversations remain searchable through LLMnesia's popup.
