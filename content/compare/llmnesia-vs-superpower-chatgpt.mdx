## Same symptom, different scope

Superpower ChatGPT (100,000+ users, 4.5★ on the Chrome Web Store) is one of the most feature-rich ChatGPT enhancement extensions available. The key word is *ChatGPT* — every feature it offers lives inside chatgpt.com.

LLMnesia works at the level of your entire AI workflow. It indexes sessions from ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and other supported platforms and provides a unified search interface. The value scales with how many AI tools you use.

## What Superpower ChatGPT does well

Superpower packs a large feature set into one extension:

- **Folders and subfolders** — organise ChatGPT conversations with colour-coded folders, drag-and-drop.
- **Search and highlight** — search through all previous ChatGPT chats with highlighted results.
- **Export** — export chats to PDF, .txt, .json, or .md.
- **Prompt chains** — save and run a series of prompts with one click.
- **Model switcher** — change GPT model mid-conversation.
- **Prompt Optimizer (Pro)** — one-click AI-powered prompt improvement.
- **Notes (Pro)** — save notes per chat, synced across devices.
- **Auto-delete / auto-archive** — automatically manage old conversations.
- **Right-click menu** — run custom prompts on any webpage.

These are well-executed features for heavy ChatGPT users. The extension is actively maintained and widely used.

## The hard limit: ChatGPT only

Every Superpower feature stops at the chatgpt.com boundary. If you run a question through Claude and get a useful answer, Superpower cannot find it. If you use Gemini for research, that history is invisible to Superpower's search. As more teams adopt multiple AI tools, this single-platform ceiling becomes a daily friction point.

## Where LLMnesia is different

- **Multi-platform coverage** — one search index spanning ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and more.
- **Background indexing** — automatic, no in-product configuration required.
- **Jump-back links** — results link directly to the original conversation on whichever platform it came from.
- **Local-first** — your index stays on your device, not dependent on any platform's UI or API.

## Choosing between them

**Use Superpower ChatGPT if:**
- Your AI workflow is exclusively inside ChatGPT.
- You want deep UI enhancements — folders, model switcher, prompt chains, notes.
- You want features beyond retrieval: exports, voice mode, GPT Store browsing.

**Use LLMnesia if:**
- You use ChatGPT alongside Claude, Gemini, or other AI tools.
- Your retrieval problem spans platforms, not just one product.
- You want a search layer that works regardless of which AI tool you used.

**Use both if:**
- You are a heavy ChatGPT user who also works in other AI platforms and wants strong organisation within ChatGPT *and* cross-platform retrieval.
