## Typical founder pattern

Founders ask similar questions repeatedly: positioning, pricing, priority, hiring, and messaging. Without retrieval, each week starts from partial memory.

## Workflow recommendation

- Use AI for rapid exploration.
- Capture final decision anchors.
- Retrieve before re-prompting.

## Expected outcome

The goal is fewer repeated strategy loops and faster movement from idea to decision to execution.
